FAQ

  • Where do prices come from? — Live prices are fetched from CoinGecko, Yahoo Finance, Finnhub, and Frankfurter APIs.
  • Why are some prices labeled "estimate"? — Items like Big Mac, Lamborghini, and real estate use approximate market prices that are updated periodically.
  • Does the app collect my data? — No. InBitcoin collects zero personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
  • Can I request a new asset? — Yes! Email us and we'll consider adding it in a future update.
